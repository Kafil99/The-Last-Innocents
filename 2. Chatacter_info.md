## Characters Overview

**Aren Vale**  
The main character. Brave but slightly reckless, often driven by emotion rather than caution.

**Mira Vale**  
Aren’s older sister, eleven years his senior. More a mother figure than a sibling, she raised and protected him with quiet strength.

**Lyra Hale**  
Aren’s childhood friend and first love. One year older than him, gentle, responsible, and naturally protective of Aren.

**Yuta Hale**  
Lyra’s younger brother, three years her junior. Playful and energetic, always teasing his sister.

**Prince Kaelros**  
The ruthless prince of the Eastern Kingdom. Power-hungry and cruel, he seized the throne through bloodshed and shows no mercy to anyone in his way.

**Old Mrs. Gable**  
A kind elderly woman with a vegetable garden. She often watches Aren and Lyra pass by, wishing them a bright future.

**Old Man Manus**  
The village baker. Owner of the local bakery, supplying bread, butter, and milk to the people of Oakhaven.
